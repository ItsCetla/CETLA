Good to know

Questions, answered

Is Every Teammate Counts a therapy provider? +
No. ETC connects athletes with independent, licensed professionals who applied and were approved by our team. When you book, you're booking directly with the professional — sessions, payment, and care all happen between you and them.
Does it cost anything to use this site? +
Browsing professionals and watching athlete stories is completely free. If you book a session, any fees are set by and paid to the professional directly — ETC never handles payment.
Do I need an account? +
No account needed. Browse, watch, and book without signing up for anything.
I'm a parent or coach — can I help my athlete connect? +
Absolutely. Many athletes take the first step because someone in their corner pointed the way. Browse professionals together, or share a profile with your athlete so they can book when they're ready.
What if I need help right now? +
If you or someone you know is in crisis, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, US) — free, confidential, and available 24/7. ETC is not an emergency service.
